Technology

The science behind the pump

REINVENTING THE BEER PUMP

We spent 10 months developing our product with three objectives in mind:
to make it convenient, to make it easy to use, and to make it fun.

Every component was studied separately, undergoing countless iterations of brainstorming, prototyping and testing.

THE AIR PUMP

Designed on 6XXX anodized alloy, this CNC machined component generates moderate air pressure inside the growler, making the beer flow through the pipe. The air pump eliminates the need of single-use compressed gas capsules, also avoiding the need of complex pressure regulators.

THE PIPE

It's the only component in touch with the beer. Of course this must be on 304 food grade stainless steel. No plastic, no aluminium, no valves along the pipe, minimizing risk of bacteria colonies.
Constant section trough the full length, ensuring minimum beer turbulence.
Optimized length and diameter to avoid beer overflow.

THE STOPPER

Designed in cad and 3D printed countless times, every single curb of this stopper has a purpose, allowing the pump to be used in more than 90% of the Growlers and Kegs on the market.
For serial mass production, high strength food grade silicone was selected.

THE AIR BLEEDER

Taking a radical approach, all valves were removed and replaced by an air bleeder, that is never in contact with the beer.
By pressing the red button the over pressure in the growler is released and the beer flow is stopped.
This mechanism minimizes risk of bacteria propagation.
