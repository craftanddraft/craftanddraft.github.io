The beer industry is responsible for more than 250 billion single-use cans and bottles annually

We need to change this

Craft beers are the best choice for those people looking for high quality and taste diversity.
Everyday more and more people switch from highly industrialized products to carefully-crafted small-batches local beer brands.

Growlers are the perfect allies, allowing zero-waste beer consumption. But we still have three problems to solve: growler weight, carbonation loss when pouring, and thermal insulation

Meet Craft & Draft,
the ultimate beer pump for your draft beer anywhere

UNIVERSAL BEER PUMP

This is the most versatile and minimalist beer pump for take-away draft beer. The exclusive stopper design makes the pump compatible with more than 90% of conventional kegs and growlers on the market.

Learn more

UNIQUE DESIGN

Our one of a kind lower pressure system eliminates the need for extra valves and disposable canisters currently seen on the conventional growler taps dominating the market, instead consumers can make use of our aesthetic, exclusive and minimalist alternative.

Learn more

STRAIGHTFORWARD AND PRACTICAL MECHANISM WITH SAFETY STOP

Our new tool is fool-proof: simply insert the pump and the pipe in the stopper, place it in the neck of your growler and you're all set. Gently pumping the handle a few times is enough to set your beer magically flowing, and should you wish to stop - just a touch of the red button will put the whole system to an instant stop.
