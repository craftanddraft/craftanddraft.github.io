CRAFT BEER LOVERS EVERYWHERE

Craft beers are the best choice for those people looking for high quality and taste diversity.
Everyday more and more people switch from highly industrialized products to carefully-crafted small-batches local beer brands.
The glower is the perfect match for enjoying local craft beer at home. The re-filling strategy offers small producers the possibility of selling their beers without the need of expensive bottling lines, democratizing the takeaway market and giving the consumer a large variety of choices.

Introducing Craft & Draft, the ultimate universal beer pump, for everyone, everywhere.

UNIVERSAL BEER PUMP

The exclusive stopper design makes the pump compatible with more than 90% of conventional kegs and growlers on the market.

UNIQUE DESIGN

Our one of a kind lower pressure system eliminates the need for extra valves seen on the conventional growlers tap currently dominating the market, allowing us to create this exclusive minimalist alternative solution.

STRAIGHTFORWARD AND PRACTICAL MECHANISM WITH SAFETY STOP

There's no chance to do it wrong. Insert the pump and the pipe in the stopper, place it in the neck of your growler and you're all set. Simply pull the pump up and down a couple of times to see your beer magically flowing. In case you want to stop, just press the red button.

ECO FRIENDLY

Zero waste. Available everywhere. No more one-use containers. Comes in two environmentally friendly packaging options: recycled kraft paper or pine wood.

WE GO EVERYWHERE

Our goal is to reach as much people as we can. We ship globally. We also take bulk orders with customized sleeves for your local brew house.
