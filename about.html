About Us

Long story short

The idea

Ever wish it would be possible to serve your favourite craft beer at home or anywhere without using complicated gear or generic lager kegs? We believe it is.

With that in mind, we came up with the idea of a simple, straightforward, easy-to-clean, easy to store and hassle-free gadget.

A small pump that pulls air inside the growler and gets the beer out of a medical-grade stainless steel pipe, attached through a silicone stopper that fits inside almost any existing growler in the market - it also features an emergency stop button that brings an instant end to the flow, just in case!

Meet the team

We are an interdisciplinary team of young professionals bringing together a variety of skills under a united passion for our work and good beer!

We believe we can make a better world with eco-friendly and creative ideas and solutions to everyday needs.
