About Us

Long story short

The idea

Would it be possible to serve your favorite craft beer at home or anywhere without using complicated gear? We believe it is.

With that in mind we came up with the idea of a simple, straightforward, easy-to-clean, store and hustle-free gadget.

A small pump that pulls air inside the growler and gets beer out of a medical grade stainless steel pipe, attached throgh a silicone stopper that fits inside almost any existing growler in the market. And of course, an early termination button that instantly stops the pouring, just in case you need it.

Meet the team

We are an interdisciplinary team of young professionals with great passion in our work.
We believe we can make better world with eco-friendly and creative ideas and solutions to everyday needs.
