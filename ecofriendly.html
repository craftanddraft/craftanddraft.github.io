Eco-friendly

We care about the environment

We designed the pump from the bottom up with every consideration for the environment from manufacturing to end of life in mind.

Our aim is to be a trailblazer in the takeaway growler trend, and in turn further develop more eco-conscious, enjoyable and streamlined products and methods to reduce waste and carbon footprint across the industry.

MASS PRODUCTION

The beer industry alone is producing over 250 billion cans and bottles annually; this is equivalent to the CO2 emissions of more than 40 million regular cars.

BOTTLES

One in every three bottles will fail to be recycled at the end of life stage, where it instead sits in landfill, contributing to unnecessary detrimental effects on the environment.

ALUMINUM CANS

It can take up to 200 years for an aluminium can to degrade in landfill or the ocean.
