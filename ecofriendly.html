Eco-friendly

We care about the environment

We designed and built this product with the best for our environment in mind

We are convinced that promoting the use of take-away growlers by enhancing the user experience would be a key move toward further developing the trend in draft beer consumption, in turn reducing waste and carbon footprint across the industry.

MASS PRODUCTION

The beer industry is producing over 250 billion cans and bottles annually; this is equivalent to the CO2 emissions of more than 40 Million cars.

BOTTLES

One in every three bottles is not being recycled at the end of life stage, instead ending up discarded in landfill and contributing to unneccesary detrimental effects on the environment.

ALUMINUM CANS

It can take up to 200 years for an aluminum can to degrade in landfill or the ocean.
